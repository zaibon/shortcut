# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Individual Creators & Bloggers**: sharing clean, short links across social media.
- **General Users**: seeking quick, hassle-free, one-off link shortening.

## Product Purpose
Shortcut provides a clean, fast, and friendly URL shortening service, with a dashboard to view link statistics and manage subscriptions. Success is a highly intuitive tool where shortening is instant, and tracking/analytics are easy to parse at a glance.

## Brand Personality
- Clean, minimalist, and developer-friendly (similar to Vercel/Linear).
- Efficient, focused, and trustworthy.
- 3-Word Personality: Minimalist, Efficient, Precise.

## Anti-references
- **Loud SaaS clichés**: Avoid aggressive gradients, massive cards, over-designed hero-metric templates, and bloated illustrations.
- **Cluttered Portals**: Avoid ad-heavy or text-heavy bookmarks, busy widgets, or distracting sidebars.

## Design Principles
- **No Friction**: The main action (shortening a link) should be immediate and require zero thought or navigation.
- **Data Clarity**: Analytics should present clear visual signals without overwhelming the user. Don't hide the core metrics in complex metrics dashboards.
- **Sleek Utility**: Keep styling understated. Use clean lines, generous breathing room, and high contrast over decorative elements.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Focus on basic contrast and usability: clear, readable typography with standard hierarchy, and legible interactive elements.
